Professional Application Made Easy, Pure & Safe

Suggested Use
Lightly squeeze the tube so that foundation flows through the sponge. Apply foundation directly onto face using the attached sponge applicator and blend evenly until desired coverage is achieved. After use, replace cap onto tube.
