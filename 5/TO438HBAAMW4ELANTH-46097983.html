รายละเอียดของสินค้า Too Faced Cocoa Contour Chiseled to Perfection

What it is:
An exclusive face contouring and highlighting kit to enhance and minimize facial features in just a few simple steps. 

What it does:
Look instantly slimmer, get lifted cheekbones, or sculpt your features like an A-list celebrity with the Cocoa Contour Chiseled to Perfection. Perfect for wearing separately or blending together, this curated kit provides two essential sculpting shades, a matte highlighter, and a luminous light shade for a contoured look that stands out. Infused with the antioxidant-rich, cocoa complex, these powders layer together seamlessly and indulge you with a decadent chocolate scent. This kit features Too Faced's Contour Buki Brush for the most professional sculpting effect, and it includes its signature Glamour Guide with three contour looks to get you started. 

This set contains: 
- 0.17 oz/ 5 mL Highlighter shade 
- 2 x 0.17 oz/ 5 mL Sculpting shade 
- 0.17 oz/ 5 mL Contouring shade 
- Contour Buki Brush 
- Signature Glamour Guide 

What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens
- Sulfates 
- Phthalates 

What else you need to know:
Too Faced Co-Founder and Chief Creative Officer Jerrod Blandino was inspired to infuse antioxidant-rich cocoa in makeup while getting a chocolate facial at a Hawaiian spa. He learned of chocolate’s benefits to the skin and included the ingredient in Too Faced’s bestselling products. 

This product is vegan-friendly, gluten-free, and cruelty-free. 

Select your favorite palette (sold separately). The Light to Medium palette includes a highlighter shade in Light Cocoa, sculpting shades in Medium Cocoa and Dark Cocoa, and a contouring shade in Pop of Light. The Medium to Deep palette features a contour shade in Cocoa Butter, sculpting shades in Medium Mocha and Deep Mocha, and a highlighter shade in Cocoa Glow.
